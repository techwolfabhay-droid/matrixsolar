Solar Energy Solutions

Complete Solar Project Details

Overview

Matrix Energy provides end-to-end solar installation for residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural sectors. MNRE certified and PM-KUSUM registered — all installations meet DISCOM and HAREDA standards. We handle everything from site survey to final net metering approval.

Sectors We Serve

Residential
Home & Housing Society
Rooftop systems for individual homes, bungalows, villas and housing societies. 1 KW to 20 KW. MNRE subsidy eligible. Grid-tied and hybrid options available.
Commercial
Shops, Offices & Institutions
Rooftop and ground-mount systems for commercial buildings, malls, schools, hospitals and offices. 10 KW to 200 KW. HAREDA and net metering compliant.
Industrial
Factories, Plants & Warehouses
Large-scale solar installations from 50 KW to 500 KW+ for industries and manufacturing units. Reduces captive power costs and qualifies for accelerated depreciation benefits.
Agricultural
Farms & PM-KUSUM Scheme
Solar water pumps and ground-mount systems for farms. PM-KUSUM registered — up to 90% subsidy for farmers. Full documentation and DISCOM approval handled by us.

System Types — What's Right for You?

On-Grid System
Connected to electricity grid. No batteries needed. Electricity goes to grid, you earn credits (net metering). Best ROI — payback in 4–6 years. Ideal for homes and offices.
Off-Grid System
100% independent from grid. Runs on solar and battery storage. Best for remote farms, villages, and areas with frequent power cuts. No electricity bill at all.
Hybrid System
Grid and battery backup combined. Use solar first, store excess in battery, then draw from grid only if needed. Ideal for hospitals, schools and critical loads.
PM-KUSUM Solar Pump
Government scheme for farmers — up to 90% subsidy on solar water pumps. Matrix handles full documentation, DISCOM approval and subsidy processing.
Ground Mount Farm
50 KW to 500 KW+ solar farms on open land. Pile-driven or concrete structure. Suitable for industries, large farms and commercial power sale.
Solar Street Lights
Standalone solar LED street lights. No trenching, no electricity bill, no monthly cost. Perfect for villages, housing societies and industrial roads.

Key Components in Every Solar System

Solar Panels
Convert sunlight to DC electricity. Warranted for 25 years. We use only Tier-1 BIS-certified panels from Waaree, Adani, Vikram, Longi and Trina.
Solar Inverter
Converts DC to AC power for use in home/office. Brands: Growatt, Solis, ABB, Delta, Fronius. 5-year warranty standard.
Mounting Structure
GI/Aluminium frames for rooftop; hot-dip galvanised pile-driven structure for ground mount. Wind-load tested up to 150 km/h.
DC & AC Cables
ISI-certified Polycab/Havells cables. UV-resistant DC cable for outdoor runs. Proper cable trays and earthing for safety compliance.
MCB / ACDB / DCDB
AC and DC distribution boxes with surge protection, fuses, and isolators. Schneider/Havells MCBs. Mandatory for safety and DISCOM compliance.
Net Meter
Bidirectional meter installed by DISCOM after approval. Records units exported to grid — you earn credits on your electricity bill.

Petrol Pump Infrastructure

Complete Pump Setup Details

Overview

Matrix Energy provides complete turnkey petrol pump setup across Haryana, Rajasthan and Delhi NCR. Experienced with BPCL, HPCL, and IOC — every project executed as per their specific design standards and layout guidelines. From structural canopy to final electrical commissioning, we handle it all with our own trained crew — no subcontractors.

What We Set Up — All Components

🏗️
Canopy Fabrication & Installation
MS/GI structure canopy designed as per oil company layout (BPCL/HPCL/IOC specification). Includes fabrication, powder coating, signage & branding panels. Wind-load compliant design.
🛣️
RCC Driveway Construction
M25/M30 grade reinforced concrete driveway with proper slope and drainage. Includes sub-base compaction, steel mesh reinforcement, and surface finish as per oil company specs.
⚡
HT/LT Electrical Panel
Complete MLDB (Main LT Distribution Board), SLDB (Sub LT Distribution Board), power factor correction panel. Schneider/ABB MCBs, ELCB, surge arrestors. Fully compliant with BPCL/HPCL/IOC norms.
🔌
Underground & Surface Wiring
ISI-certified Polycab/Havells armoured cables for underground runs. Dispenser wiring, canopy lighting wiring, office wiring — all laid in conduit/cable tray as per safety norms.
💡
Yard Pole & Canopy Lighting
High-mast yard pole lights for forecourt illumination. LED canopy lights, under-canopy diffusers. Lighting designed for uniform lux levels as required by oil companies and safety standards.
🏎️
DG Set Installation
Kirloskar/Cummins/Mahindra diesel generator sets from 25 KVA to 250 KVA. Includes AMF panel, ATS changeover switch, acoustic enclosure, fuel tank & exhaust system. Full load testing done.
🌬️
Air Compressor & Air Gauge
Compressed air system for tyre inflation — Elgi/Kirloskar air compressor, auto cut-off pressure switch, airline piping, digital air gauges at each island. Mandatory for modern pump setups.
⚙️
Servo Motor / Dispenser Wiring
Electrical connection and commissioning of fuel dispensers (Gilbarco, Wayne, Tatsuno). Servo motor panel wiring, dispenser island wiring, earthing & bonding as per PESO regulations.
🔧
Earthing & Lightning Protection
Chemical earthing pits with GI/copper electrodes. Lightning arrester masts. Proper equipotential bonding of all metallic structures. Mandatory for PESO license compliance at fuel stations.
🏢
Office & Utility Electrical Work
Office wiring, air conditioning points, CCTV power points, UPS installation. Signage power supply. Staff room, toilet & store electrical work as part of complete pump setup.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Most common questions about our solar and petrol pump services — answered simply.

How long does it take to install a solar plant?
A typical rooftop solar system (1 KW – 50 KW) is installed within 5–10 working days. This includes site survey, design, material supply and complete installation. Larger ground mount or commercial projects (50 KW+) may take 15–30 days depending on site conditions and DISCOM paperwork.
How much subsidy is available under PM-KUSUM scheme?
Under PM-KUSUM scheme, farmers receive 60–90% subsidy on solar pumps (Central + State combined). Matrix Energy is a registered PM-KUSUM installer and handles all documentation — DPR, application, DISCOM approval, and subsidy disbursal. You just need to provide your land details and bank account.
What is the ROI (return on investment) period for solar?
On-grid (grid-tied) systems typically have a payback period of 4–6 years, after which you get free electricity for 20–25 years. Off-grid systems take slightly longer — 6–8 years — due to battery costs. PM-KUSUM subsidy pumps pay back in just 2–3 years. We provide a free ROI calculation with every estimate.
How long does petrol pump canopy and driveway work take?
A standard petrol pump canopy fabrication and installation completes in 10–15 days. RCC driveway construction takes 7–10 days (including curing time). Electrical panel + wiring takes 5–7 days. If all work is done together — canopy, driveway, electrical, DG set — the entire project is handed over in 3–5 weeks. We work strictly as per BPCL/HPCL/IOC layout standards.
Does Matrix Energy provide AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract)?
Yes, Matrix Energy provides comprehensive AMC. For solar systems: inverter performance monitoring, panel cleaning, wiring checks, and 24/7 emergency helpline. For petrol pump clients: 6-monthly electrical safety inspection, DG set servicing, and panel audit. AMC clients receive priority response — field team visit guaranteed within 4 hours.
Do you work outside Haryana?
Yes! Matrix Energy currently operates in Haryana, Rajasthan and Delhi NCR. In Rajasthan, we have completed significant work in Alwar, Sikar, Jhunjhunu and border districts. Delhi NCR petrol pump infrastructure projects have also been successfully completed. For any location, we first confirm feasibility with a site visit.
Is the quote and site survey free? Any commitment required?
Completely free — no charges, no commitment required. Just fill the form on this page or WhatsApp us. We will call/WhatsApp you within 24 hours. Consultation, site survey and detailed quotation are all free of cost. You decide after receiving the quote — no pressure at all.
